Bedroom & Living Areas (Standard Service)

Keep your living spaces fresh and comfortable with our standard cleaning service.

Our standard bedroom and living area cleaning is designed for normally maintained homes across NYC. We focus on visible dust, surface care, floor cleaning, and light straightening to keep your daily spaces neat, clean, and comfortable.

What This Standard Service Covers

This service includes essential upkeep for bedrooms and living areas, helping you maintain a clean and welcoming home environment with consistent standard cleaning.

This Service Includes:

Bedroom & Living Areas

  • Dusting all accessible surfaces, furniture, and decor
  • Cleaning mirrors and glass surfaces
  • Making beds (if linens are provided and accessible)
  • Vacuuming carpets, rugs, and floors (client must provide a vacuum if required)
  • Mopping hard floors
  • Light straightening of visible areas
  • Removing visible dust and debris
  • Taking out the trash

Important Notes

Deep dusting, heavy buildup, or neglected areas are not included
Moving heavy furniture is not included
Interior closet cleaning is not included
Laundry or folding clothes is not included
Organization or decluttering is not included
Additional time or service upgrade may be required for cluttered or heavily used spaces

What’s Not Included in Standard Cleaning

To ensure transparency, the following services are not included in standard cleaning and can be added separately:

  • Inside refrigerator
  • Inside oven
  • Inside cabinets and drawers
  • Washing dishes
  • Laundry or folding clothes
  • Interior window cleaning
  • Heavy grease, soap scum, or deep buildup removal
  • Mold or mildew removal
  • Organization or decluttering
  • Moving heavy furniture

Important Notice

The final price depends on the size and condition of the home. Standard cleaning is intended for normally maintained spaces. If the home requires additional work or deep cleaning, we will notify you before proceeding and provide options.
