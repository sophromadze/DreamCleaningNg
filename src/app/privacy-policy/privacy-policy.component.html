8.2 Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following categories of cookies:

  • Necessary / Essential Cookies (Type: Session Cookies) — These cookies are essential to provide you with services available through our Website and to enable you to use some of its features. They help authenticate users and prevent fraudulent use of user accounts. Without these cookies, the services you have requested cannot be provided.
  • Cookie Notice Acceptance Cookies (Type: Persistent Cookies) — These cookies identify whether you have accepted the use of cookies on our Website, so you are not prompted repeatedly.
  • Functionality Cookies (Type: Persistent Cookies) — These cookies allow us to remember choices you make when you use our Website, such as remembering your login details, language preference, or booking preferences. The purpose is to provide you with a more personalized experience and to avoid you having to re-enter your preferences each time you visit.
  • Analytics and Performance Cookies (Type: Persistent Cookies) — These cookies, including those set by Microsoft Clarity and similar services, help us understand how visitors interact with our Website by collecting and reporting information about page visits, time on site, and user behavior patterns. This data is used to improve our Website and services.
