Our Story

We started Dream Cleaning in 2024 because we noticed a common problem in NYC's cleaning industry — too many companies focused on volume over quality, and customers were left with inconsistent, unreliable service. We built Dream Cleaning around one mission: put people first by offering personalized, reliable, and high-quality cleaning tailored to each client's space and lifestyle.

Today, our team of 10+ experienced professionals serves homes and offices across three boroughs. We've cleaned over 300 apartments, brownstones, and offices — and our 5.0-star Google rating reflects the trust and care we bring to every job.
