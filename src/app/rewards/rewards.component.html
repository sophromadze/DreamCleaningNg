How It Works

Earning Points

Every dollar you spend on a cleaning earns you {{ summary.guide.pointsPerDollar | number:'1.0-1' }} bubble points. Your tier multiplies this:

  • Bubble tier — {{ summary.guide.bubbleMultiplier }}x (base rate)
  • Super Bubble tier — {{ summary.guide.superBubbleMultiplier }}x
  • Ultra Bubble tier — {{ summary.guide.ultraBubbleMultiplier }}x

Example: a $100 cleaning at Bubble tier = {{ 100 * summary.guide.pointsPerDollar * summary.guide.bubbleMultiplier | number:'1.0-0' }} pts  |  Super Bubble = {{ 100 * summary.guide.pointsPerDollar * summary.guide.superBubbleMultiplier | number:'1.0-0' }} pts  |  Ultra Bubble = {{ 100 * summary.guide.pointsPerDollar * summary.guide.ultraBubbleMultiplier | number:'1.0-0' }} pts

Tiers

Your tier is based on your total lifetime spending with us.

  • Bubble — starts from $0
  • Super Bubble — unlock at ${{ summary.guide.tierSuperBubbleMinSpent | number:'1.0-0' }} spent
  • Ultra Bubble — unlock at ${{ summary.guide.tierUltraBubbleMinSpent | number:'1.0-0' }} spent

Higher tiers multiply every point you earn going forward.

Redeeming Points

Apply points at checkout to get a discount on your next booking:

  • {{ opt.points | number }} pts = ${{ opt.dollarValue | number:'1.0-0' }} off

Points are applied before payment and reduce your total directly.

Bonuses
  • Welcome bonus: get {{ summary.guide.welcomeBonusPoints | number }} pts when you create your account.
  • Returning customer bonus:

    After your first cleaning, you can earn extra points on future bookings based on the cleaning frequency you choose when you book — on the step “How often would you like us to clean?” It is tied to that choice for each booking, not a flat boost on every order.

    One Time cleanings are not eligible. Recurring plans are, for example: Weekly {{ summary.guide.recurringBonusPercent | number:'1.0-0' }}% extra on points for that order, Bi-Weekly 10%, and Monthly 5% — the same percentages you see on each option when you book.

  • Loyalty booster: book again within {{ summary.guide.nextOrderBoosterDays }} days of your last cleaning and earn an extra {{ summary.guide.nextOrderBoosterPercent | number:'1.0-0' }}% points.
  • Streak bonuses: book 3 cleanings in a row and earn +{{ summary.guide.streak3Bonus | number }} pts; reach 6 in a row for +{{ summary.guide.streak6Bonus | number }} pts.
  • Review bonus: leave us a Google review and earn {{ summary.guide.reviewBonusPoints | number }} pts (awarded manually by our team).
Refer a Friend

Share your referral link and both of you benefit:

  • Your friend gets the normal welcome bonus when they create an account, plus an extra {{ summary.guide.referralNewUserBonusPoints | number }} pts for signing up with your link.
  • You earn a ${{ summary.guide.referralOrderCreditAmount | number:'1.0-0' }} reward balance once your friend completes their first cleaning — apply manually on your future bookings.

There's no limit to how many friends you can refer.
